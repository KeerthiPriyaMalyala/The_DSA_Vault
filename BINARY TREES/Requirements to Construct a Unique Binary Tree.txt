Requirements to Construct a Unique Binary Tree 🌳

To construct a unique Binary Tree, we need:

✅ Any TWO traversals

But one traversal must be:

Inorder
✅ Valid Combinations
Traversal 1	Traversal 2	Unique Tree Possible?
Inorder	Preorder	✅ Yes
Inorder	Postorder	✅ Yes
Inorder	Level Order	✅ Yes
Preorder	Postorder	❌ No (not always unique)
🧠 Why Inorder is Important?

Because:

Inorder divides tree into
Left Subtree and Right Subtree

That helps uniquely identify structure.

🌳 Example
Inorder
D B E A F C
Preorder
A B D E C F
🔥 Construction Logic

From Preorder:

First element = Root

So:

Root = A

Now find A in inorder:

D B E | A | F C

Left side:

D B E

Right side:

F C

Thus tree becomes uniquely determined 😄

❌ Why Preorder + Postorder alone fails?

Because multiple trees can produce same traversals.

Example:

Preorder  : A B
Postorder : B A

Possible Trees:

A        A
/          \
B            B

Both give same traversals 😄

🧠 Final Remember Trick
Inorder + anything = Unique BT ✅
Without inorder = Not guaranteed ❌
